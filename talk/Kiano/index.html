Kiano

中文

What is Kiano?

Kiano is a mix of "Keyboard" and "Piano", it means you can use your own keyboard to play a wonderful music!

Kiano uses the buzzer of the computer to make sound, so you should make sure that your computer has buzzer before you use Kiano.

Where to Download?

You can click here to download the binary file of Kiano, and you can also choose to download the cpp code.

How to Use?

Just double click the Kiano.exe, then you'll find that you computer will beep as you press different key!

In the normal mode, you can press 1-8 to play do,re,mi,fa,so,la,si and DO, press 9 to go up 7 steps, press 0 to go down 7 steps, and when you press A-Z, the computer will beep in random frequency

When you click the mouse wheel, the mode will change between the normal mode and the advanced mode.

In the advanced mode, there are some special keys to control Kiano

Q(Quit) - Quit the program

R(Ring) - Ring the bell

D(Down) - Play the sound that will gradually become faded

U(Up) - Play the sound that will gradually become soaring
